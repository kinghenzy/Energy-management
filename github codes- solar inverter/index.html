Solar PV Sizing & Blueprint Calculations

Step-by-Step
Clear English Solar Sizing Formulas:

1. Adjust for Real-World Losses:

Loss-Adjusted Demand = Daily kWh Demand multiplied by 1.25 safety factor

The 1.25 multiplier compensates for thermodynamic heat, inverter conversion, and dirty panels.

2. Determine Array Wattage Needs:

Required Array Size = (Loss-Adjusted Demand divided by Peak Sun Hours) multiplied by 1,000

3. Calculate Panel Quantity:

Total Panels Needed = Required Array Size divided by Single Panel Wattage (rounded up)
